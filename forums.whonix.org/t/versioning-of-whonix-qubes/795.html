Versioning of Whonix Qubes

Qubes-Whonix
WhonixQubes January 13, 2015, 2:53am #1

Being consciously aware of the versioning between different releases of Whonix Qubes is important for helping us test, support, and develop the system.

Since the new Whonix Qubes has been incorporated into the Qubes repos, the releases published by the Qubes team are likely to be the default releases that we support.

[hr]

Whonix Qubes Binary Release Versioning

So far, as of this posting, there has only been one Whonix binary release by the Qubes team, which includes two VM templates (Whonix-Gateway and Whonix-Workstation).

These initial current template version numbers are:

  • Whonix-Gateway: 2.1.7-201412012134

  • Whonix-Workstation: 2.1.7-201412012203

They also come with corresponding SHA256 hash checksums:

  • Whonix-Gateway: ad73c465b86dea8b9a90c733585d36b4ed9c1c5dce2b7997f29bd9782921bea6

  • Whonix-Workstation: 3737e071d8a8085c47bae35d85d9289a77dadcac83de5866a7a5d016060964ba

The binary RPM package filenames are:

  • Whonix-Gateway: qubes-template-whonix-gateway-experimental-2.1.7-201412012134.noarch.rpm

  • Whonix-Workstation: qubes-template-whonix-workstation-experimental-2.1.7-201412012203.noarch.rpm
