Whonix is Whonix, Tails is Tails .
Whonix save data on the disk if you want, Tails not if you want .
Try to understand this .
With Whonix you can choose to save data or not thanks to Virtualbox for example, Tails let save the data with persistence feature but it’s a different thing, definetely Whonix is better .

However i advise you do not install Whonix to SD, it will be too slow . Use a USB 3.0 external HDD, make a FDE ( Full DIsk Encryption ), install Whonix in other hidden partition and enjoy .
If you don’t want to save data in your Whonix, search anonguide to Google and read all chapters .
They will explain how to make a “live” Whonix, better than a normal distro live conception .
