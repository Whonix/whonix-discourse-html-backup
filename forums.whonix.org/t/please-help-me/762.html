hi, first of all note the whonix must be in one virtualbox , you cant have one guest in one virtualbox and the other in other virtualbox , and you cant have to two virtualbox installed on same host , for usability reasons (performance) need usb 3 supported by motherboard and a usb 3 flash with at least 200 mb/s read/write ,it is bad idea to have a operating system on a usb2 devive because usb2 have max 35 mb/s read /write speeds , and that will decrease the performance of system dramaticaly. so that usb 3 flash must have and at least 16 gb capacity if one of the two guest of whonix will be there and if that will be linux , because windows need more space . after all you must have a cpu with vt-x suport and motherboard with that again , otherwise you will run very slowly whonix beliave me , if you have not that (vt-x) you can use tails with persistance on usb , that support to obfs3 bridges and have stream isolation , its the right after whonix , but us i tell you , if you have not vt-x its better to use tails persistance , for performance reasons .
