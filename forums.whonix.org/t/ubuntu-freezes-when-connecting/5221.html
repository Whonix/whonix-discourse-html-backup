This sound like a RAM issue. Do you have sufficient RAM for both Whonix and your host running at the same time? Try shutting down any unnecessary processes on your host.

You can also use rads to decrease the RAM in Whonix-Gateway to the minimum necessary for the desktop to start: 512 MB
