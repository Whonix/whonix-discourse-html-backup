Good day,

first of all, it is impossible, to use Whonix with Tails. Furthermore, what do you mean by “using Whonix over the Tor Browser Bundle”? The TBB is included with Whonix inside the Workstation and should there and only there be used. Furthermore, changes to it are not recommended (if you aren’t 100% knowledged in what you do), outside of using the, by you mentioned, sliders. And, to answer your last question, someone using Whonix in a proper configuration is far safer than someone using the TBB on host. Like I’ve said, Whonix uses the TBB itself, though in an isolated workstation, were IP leaks are impossible.

Good day,

first of all, without wanting to offend anyone, the people at DDW aren’t the “most trustworthy experts” in their field. Not a long time ago, they actually published a guide in which they recommended using the Google (!) 2FA android app, over a GPG based 2FA solution, which was probably one of the worst tips they could ever give anyone. Whonix however, does offer a few more security features over Tails or the TBB on host, we actually made a comparison here: https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Comparison_with_Others There, everything is, in detail, explained. Also, using Whonix over VBox isn’t intrinsignley unsafer, than for example KVM, however, it is “less open sourcey”, which is why we are supporting alternatives as well. The only “safer” configuration, using one PC, is one using Qubes, but such a configuration is safer than KVM as well, see: https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Qubes/Why_use_Qubes_over_other_Virtualizers

Have a nice day,

Ego
