Hi,

Either:

a) Upgrade Whonix 13 to 14 following these instructions (works for both non-Qubes-Whonix and Qubes-Whonix)

http://kkkkkkkkkk63ava6.onion/wiki/Upgrading_Whonix_13_to_Whonix_14

b) Or download, verify, install new Whonix 14 images (Gateway and Workstation) for your platform in non-Qubes-Whonix from the download section when stable is released. Which will be here (edit - correct link):

http://kkkkkkkkkk63ava6.onion/wiki/Download

Oh yeah, and don’t forget to read the Whonix Release Notes for Whonix 14.

http://kkkkkkkkkk63ava6.onion/wiki/Whonix_Release_Notes
