Hi dofamin

Open sdwdate log

cat /var/log/sdwdate.log

Follow sdwdate log

tail -f /var/log/sdwdate.log

sdwdate status

sudo service sdwdate status

Reload sdwdate

sudo service sdwdate reload

Restart sdwdate

sudo service sdwdate restart

Etc…
