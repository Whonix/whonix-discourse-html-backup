Patrick April 11, 2015, 1:51pm #7

/etc/environment isn’t a folder. It’s a file and may not exist. Just create it.

Not into binary file. That will break. With TBB Alpha it’s /home/user/tor-browser_en-US/Browser/start-tor-browser. Or just run from terminal.

export TOR_TRANSPROXY=1 /home/user/tor-browser_en-US/start-tor-browser.desktop
