Patrick February 13, 2015, 5:08am #2

This is rather problematic, due to protocol leaks:
https://trac.torproject.org/projects/tor/wiki/doc/TorifyHOWTO#Protocolleaks

I.e. cookies + a “zillion” of other ways other than IP that track your pseudonym [and after you log in somewhere, also your real identity].

See also:
https://www.whonix.org/wiki/DoNot#Do_not_confuse_Anonymity_with_Pseudonymity.

And also somewhat https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Other_Operating_Systems applies here, since it is essentially a Whonix-Custom-Workstation that you are using.
