0brand August 5, 2018, 10:15pm #4

Hi McJack

When running apt-get dist-upgrade there might not always be packages that need upgrading. Everything looked OK to me.

Since Whonix is based on Debian. What you are seeing, v8 , is the version of Debian that Whonix is based on. Whonix 13 (what you are using) is based on Debian 8 jessie.

Whonix 14 is the upcoming release which is based on Debian 9 stretch. It is still in testing but will become stable shortly.

Something to consider: There are no know serious bugs and Whonix 14 is considered safer to use than Whonix 13. This is not to say that Whonix 13 is unsafe. You can still use it if you wish but it is recommend that users upgrade to Whonix 14 tester images.

Download new Whonix 14 Templates (recommended)

https://whonix.org/wiki/Upgrading_Whonix_13_to_Whonix_14#Download_New_Templates

Or upgrade in place:

https://whonix.org/wiki/Upgrading_Whonix_13_to_Whonix_14
