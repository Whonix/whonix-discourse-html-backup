madaidan October 5, 2019, 12:57pm #3

It looks like it can’t find init for some reason.

When starting the VM, when you get to the boot menu, press e before booting, scroll down to the line that starts with linux and ends with mds=full,nosmt. At the end of that line try adding init=/sbin/init and press Ctrl + X on your keyboard. Does that fix it?
