I personally wouldn’t mix them up. With such experiments you increase the likelihood of messing up something.

Likely you will have a hard time finding people who did that. However, the Whonix specificness of that question is limited. Also as per https://www.whonix.org/wiki/About#Based_on_Debian if you really want to do this, I recommend to learn first how to do something similar without involving Whonix. If you figured out, add Whonix to the mix.
