This is not possible. Or even if it was possible in theory… At very
least at time of writing switching from persistent to live mode without
reboot is unsupported.

What live indicator says can be safely ignored if you know what you are
doing. (Know you’re in which mode.)

snapd uses mount. This might confuse live mode indicator.
