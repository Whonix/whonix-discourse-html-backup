I searched through the wiki/forum and I did not see much if any feed back from Cinnamon desktop users. Just because there are not any known/reported privacy implications with Cinnamon does not mean there aren’t any serious ones. It just means that it has not been tested by the Whonix team.

On the other hand if you wanted to test the Cinnamon desktop I’m sure your feedback would be appreciated. Keep in mind that you should not use that desktop environment for anything requiring anonymity as it is unsupported. Just use for testing purposes.

You can find additional information here:

https://whonix.org/wiki/Other_Desktop_Environments
