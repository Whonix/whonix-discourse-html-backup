I can only answer the stream isolation part.

See:

Whonix – 7 May 20

Stream Isolation

Prevent Identity Correlation through Circuit Sharing by using Tor Stream Isolation

Use one of the prepared custom ports. (Read around “Without IsolateDestAddr and without IsolateDestPort: SocksPort 192.168.0.10:9152 to 9159”.)

No idea about the other part.
