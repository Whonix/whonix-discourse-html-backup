We should nail some wallpaper guidelines here from a licensing perspective. Consider this: I’m not an artist myself, but I may like to post a wallpaper submission (if I come across something suitable either by actively researching or by accident). Now, what licenses are acceptable here? I guess public domain, creative commons(?), gpl(? - if there is at all artwork licensed under gpl). others?

License:

  • should be FSF approved Free Software License (https://www.gnu.org/licenses/license-list.html)
  • doesn’t need to be original work (as long as licensing is sorted out)
  • for other stuff, see https://github.com/Whonix/Whonix/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md and feel free to open a separate topic about it
