Reason:

  • Most likely: temporary Tor failure.
  • Less likely: Debian mirror temporarily down.
  • Even less likely: something else.

Solution:

  • stop the current attempt (ctrl + c)
  • change your circuit (example using arm: https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Tor_Controller#Arm)
  • try again
