Tor 0.4.0.1 released

Development
HulaHoop January 19, 2019, 9:43pm #1

Tor 0.4.0.1 is released. A couple of interesting things are an experimental new padding scheme and a power saving dormant mode. A lot of memory and startup speed optimizations done.

https://lists.torproject.org/pipermail/tor-talk/2019-January/044826.html
