Macchanger would be more useful on the host. And a useful implementation would offer what Tails is offering only for Tails for all Debian and derivative users. Someone should generalize their work. I am afraid, it will take a very long time until I get to that.

About the graphical package manager… Well, synaptic and apper have just been removed. Because they have been proven to cause more support requests than terminal apt-get. I am afraid, I am not aware of any usable graphical package manger in the Debian world, that always works in all cases. (I am not implying the Fedora world has one, I don’t know about others.)
