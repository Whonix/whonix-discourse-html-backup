These folders don’t exist anymore, because Whonix has been split into packages. Your best bet to update these links is to search for the files after closing whole Whonix git.

For example for the link https://github.com/Whonix/Whonix/blob/master/whonix_shared/etc/kde4/kdm/kdmrc you would only search for kdmrc.

Then you find:
./packages/kde-kdm-autologin/etc/kde4/kdm/kdmrc.anondist

And that corresponding link is:
https://github.com/Whonix/kde-kdm-autologin/blob/master/etc/kde4/kdm/kdmrc.anondist
