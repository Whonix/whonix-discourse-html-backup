torjunkie January 28, 2018, 2:20am #6

When check.torproject.org goes down, so will whonixcheck functions that rely on it:

https://trac.torproject.org/projects/tor/ticket/25053

https://trac.torproject.org/projects/tor/ticket/25041

Sometimes the Debian onions are spotty - particularly http://sgvtcaew4bxjd7ln.onion. That is a debian.org issue, not a Whonix one.

If you’re worried about getting timely updates i.e. down for a day or so, just change your sources.list temporarily back to the normal “clearnet” addresses, and comment out the onions you added via the security guide instructions, then re-run the update checks.
