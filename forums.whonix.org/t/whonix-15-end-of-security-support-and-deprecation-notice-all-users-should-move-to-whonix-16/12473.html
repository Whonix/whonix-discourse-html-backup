Whonix 15 End of Security Support and Deprecation Notice - All users should move to Whonix 16!

News
important-news
Patrick October 1, 2021, 11:11am #1

As per Whonix Support Schedule, this post serves as Whonix version 15 deprecation notice.

Whonix 16 stable has been released for all platforms.

  • Qubes-Whonix 16 has been Released! (Debian 11 bullseye based) - Major Release
  • Whonix 16 has been Released! (Debian 11 bullseye based) - for VirtualBox - Major Release / Whonix 16.0.3.1 - for VirtualBox - Point Release!
  • Whonix 16.0.2.7 KVM (Debian 11 bullseye based) - Major Stable Release

Whonix 15 will be deprecated on 14 November 2021.

All users should upgrade!

The more time is passing after the deprecation date, it might get harder or even impossible to perform an in-place release upgrade.
