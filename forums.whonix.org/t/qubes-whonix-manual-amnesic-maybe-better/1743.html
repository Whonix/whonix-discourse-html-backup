Patrick December 9, 2015, 4:47pm #2

To my knowledge, at time of writing, there is no Qubes Live DVD in the works. The Qubes installer DVD is not a Live DVD.

Deleting a TemplateBasedVM is not amnesic by definition, because files are written to the disk while you use it.

And deleting a TemplateBasedVM is not amnesic either, because they are to my knowledge not securely wiped and all traces removed. [If you want to call that this way - this is a Qubes - not a Whonix issue.]

For amnesic Qubes-Whonix we need to wait for https://github.com/QubesOS/qubes-issues/issues/904 and Whonix specific fixes. (Listed here - https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Qubes/Disposable_VM - and this is also where documentation can be found later once Qubes-Whonix can be used amnesic - goal is Whonix 13.)
