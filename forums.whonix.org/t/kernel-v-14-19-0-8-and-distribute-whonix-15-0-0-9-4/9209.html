anontor March 28, 2020, 1:16am #2

I think that your issue with this particular problem (not the other one in your other thread) may be compounded by the Virtualbox version. This is because the difference in the branches: your host Virtualbox is 5.2 but Whonix is using the Guest Additions that are a part of the 6.1.x tree. I have noticed in the past that when trying to start virtual machines with a host Virtualbox version that is too far away from the version being used on the guests by Guest Additions, some strange problems can happen.
