I’m presuming you want to create your own Custom-Workstation, since you want to edit: /etc/network/interfaces We have a guide on that topic here: https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Other_Operating_Systems in which everything, which needs to be modified in /etc/network/interfaces is explained in detail.
