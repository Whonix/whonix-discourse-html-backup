Patrick June 6, 2014, 5:35pm #2

This could also just mean, that a relay is having technical difficulties or offline due to someone trying to ddos using the Tor network and therefore ddossing the Tor network itself. New identity results in using a different route, so that could explain why it works. Apart from that, there is nothing to suggest.

Generally, such issues are best discussed on the tor-talk mailing list, because Tor devs are reading it. They might be able to take counter measures, explain, etc.
