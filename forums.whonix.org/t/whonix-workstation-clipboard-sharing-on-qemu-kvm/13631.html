KVM
LordNikkon May 6, 2022, 12:55pm #1

Is there a way to enable clipboard sharing for the whonix workstation? It works fine in the gateway but I do need it enabled for workstation. I’m using virt manager with qemu + kvm.

0x1f June 28, 2022, 9:18pm #2

Sure, in Virt-Manager Edit > Preferences > check “Enable XML editing”
in workstation click on the bulb at top left > Overview > XML

Find and replace <clipboard copypaste="no"/> to <clipboard copypaste="yes"/>
