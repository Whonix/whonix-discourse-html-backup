European Union (EU) wide tax-deductible donations to Whonix are now possible

News
Patrick May 4, 2020, 3:12pm #1

In addition to already existing non-tax-deductible Whonix donations it is now possible to make European Union (EU) wide tax-deductible donations to Whonix.

This is possible thanks to Center for the Cultivation of Technology (CCT), which is a charitable non-profit host organization in Germany for international Free Software projects, which Whonix has partnered with.
