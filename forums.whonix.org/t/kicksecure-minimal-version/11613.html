renice:

Most minimal (therefore also most difficult use case, most support intensive) doesn‚Äôt have a dedicated maintainer. Even CLI as currently is hasn‚Äôt. Lack of manpower.

Instead of kicksecure-cli consider kicksecure-dependencies-cli or pick and choose packages from that.
