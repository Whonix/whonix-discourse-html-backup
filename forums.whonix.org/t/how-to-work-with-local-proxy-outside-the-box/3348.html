entr0py December 30, 2016, 5:38pm #2

Not sure I understand your configuration. You need to provide more details. Depending on the proxy, it might be difficult or even impossible because Virtualbox networking resides on OSI Layer 2 (ie doesn’t speak TCP/IP),

If it’s an SSH tunnel you are talking about, then it’s possible using instructions like this: http://ku1ik.com/2011/11/12/tunnel-vm-network-ssh.html. This is neither supported nor tested so proceed at your own risk. Generally, it is not a good idea to alter networking unless you understand what you’re doing.
