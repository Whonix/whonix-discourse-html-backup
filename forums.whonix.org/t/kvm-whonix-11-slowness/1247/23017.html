Maybe give this a try in the KVM guests…

sudo vi /usr/share/X11/xorg.conf.d/50-qxl.conf

Section "Device"
  Identifier "qxl"
  Driver "qxl"
  Option "ENABLE_SURFACES" "False"
EndSection

And reboot
