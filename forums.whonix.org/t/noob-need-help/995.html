Bonjour bullabdomen.

1: First, you install and update Qubes OS on your bare metal host computer:

  • http://www.qubes-os.org/wiki/InstallationGuideR2

If it is Step 3 you’re stuck on, then:

The “Launcher Menu” is the Linux equivalent of the “Start Menu” in Windows.

In Qubes, it is the little blue and white “Q” symbol in the lower left corner of the desktop.

For the Whonix-Gateway TemplateVM:

Launcher Menu --> Template: whonix-gateway-experimental --> Terminal

For the Whonix-Workstation TemplateVM:

Launcher Menu --> Template: whonix-workstation-gnome --> Terminal
