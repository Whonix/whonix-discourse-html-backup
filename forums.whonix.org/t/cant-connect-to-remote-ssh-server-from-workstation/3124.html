entr0py November 5, 2016, 6:06am #2

Whonix-specific:

  • Is the SSH server located on the public internet? Or on a local network (relative to your workstation)? Might be related to Whonix stream isolation which forces ssh through Tor.

Non-Whonix specific:

  • The firewall on your server is not configured correctly.
  • It’s common practice to disable root logins on SSH servers.
