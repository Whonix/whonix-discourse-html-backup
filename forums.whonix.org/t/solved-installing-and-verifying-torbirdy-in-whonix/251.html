Patrick April 28, 2014, 10:34am #4

Understanding that warning is worth it nevertheless.

On https://www.whonix.org/wiki/Verify_the_virtual_machine_images_using_the_command_line it’s written:
“This doesn’t alter the validity of the signature according to the key you downloaded. This warning rather has to do with the trust that you put in Whonix signing key and the web of trust (https://www.whonix.org/wiki/OpenPGP#The_OpenPGP_Web_of_Trust). To remove this warning you would have to personally sign Whonix signing key with your own key.”

I don’t have a better explanation for this, so I recommend reading a few other explanations attempts on the web.
