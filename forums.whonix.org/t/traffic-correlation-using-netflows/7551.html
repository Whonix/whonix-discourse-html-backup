ccc0dy:

I’m using Noisy (github /1tayH/noisy) on Workstation

Another useless measure that wastes resources. Creating chaff to hide the wheat is a more complex problem then simplycontinuously generating bogus traffic. Website fingerprinting would make it trivial to figure out that the requested data is the same fake request. Recent padding implementation in Tor intelligently sends padded cells in just the right amount to obscure traffic from malicious Tor nodes while not wasting bandwidth. Noisy is redundant at best.
