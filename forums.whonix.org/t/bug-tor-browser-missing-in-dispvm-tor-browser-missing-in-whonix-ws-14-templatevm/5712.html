Patrick August 27, 2018, 11:19pm #4

Fix landed in Whonix 14 testers repository.

However

  • for users who are affected by this bug: will only take effect when https://github.com/Whonix/tb-updater/blob/master/usr/share/tb-updater/tbb_hardcoded_version gets updated the next time, i.e. after a new TBB version gets released. So meanwhile please use update-torbrowser in whonix-ws-14 TemplateVM
  • for users who reinstall and get the upgrade before this bug happens, these will not be affacted (although this is no reason at all for reinstallation)
