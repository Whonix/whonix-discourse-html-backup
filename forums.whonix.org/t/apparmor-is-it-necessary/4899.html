What are the advantages? What does it change? How is it configured. What are the risks of running Whonix with KVM without AppArmor?

Its configured automatically by the security extension of libvirt called sVirt. The advnatage is it wraps the entire guest with its arbitrary programs in a protective apparmor layer. There is no performance penalty and no conflicts with configured shared folders. I don’t uderstand why you wouldn’t want it.
