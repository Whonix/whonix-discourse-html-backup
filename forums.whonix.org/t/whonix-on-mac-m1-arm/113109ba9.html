mount --bind /dev "${mpoint_os}/dev"

Installing grub inside an image, a chroot is difficult. Most instructions mount the host‚Äôs /dev inside the chroot. Not an immediate security risk but the image chroot has no business in the host‚Äôs real /dev. Best avoided if possible.

/usr/sbin/grml-debootstrap uses instead:

That might be a faster and more secure solution.
