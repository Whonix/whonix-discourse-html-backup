Good day,

While it’s nice to see, that you could solve your problem, let me say that using Skype over TOR is maybe not in your best interest. Please read the following, to understand why this may create some issues of its own: https://www.whonix.org/wiki/DoNot What applies to Facebook here applies to Skype as well, especially because you transmit your voice over it and it’s a proprietary software. Furthermore, using something like Skype over the TOR-Network simply is not a good idea, since the possible traffic on there is by design limited and shouldn’t be used up by things like P2P or VOIP. If you’re only interested in text communication, I would recommend a good OTR.
