NotGoldButRich:

Its important to setup vpn on win 10 guest.

A VPN installed in a Whonix-Custom-Workstation (running windows 10) (or does not matter also if using Whonix-Default-Workstation) conceptually cannot hide Tor from internet service provier (ISP). That is because all connections origination from the workstation are routed through Tor. The workstation conceptually has no control over what Tor is going to do with the traffic.

A Separate VPN-Gateway such as

  • Whonix-Workstation → Whonix-Gateway → VPN-Gateway
  • User → VPN → Tor → Internet

is unsupported with Non-Qubes-Whonix.
